Trackunit Setup Guide

For United Rentals customers using Trackunit hardware and telematics services

Getting Started

This page walks you through installing, activating, and verifying your Trackunit device provided through United Rentals. Follow the steps below to ensure your equipment is reporting correctly.

1. Device Installation

✔ Mount the Device

Secure the Trackunit to a stable surface on the equipment. Ensure GPS can see the sky and avoid placing it near heavy metal shielding.

✔ Connect Power

Attach the power cable to the equipment’s battery or ignition source. Confirm the wiring follows the provided wiring diagram for your specific device model.

✔ Check LED Indicators

A solid or blinking green LED usually confirms power and connectivity. Refer to your device sheet for exact LED behavior.

2. Activation

✔ Provide Asset Information

Ensure United Rentals or your Trackunit account representative has the correct serial number, equipment ID, and location.

✔ Wait for First Signal

The device should report within 5–15 minutes once powered. Movement or engine-on events help trigger initial data.

3. Verification

✔ Confirm Data in Trackunit Manager

Log in to your Trackunit dashboard and verify GPS location, runtime, and equipment status are updating.

✔ Troubleshooting

If no data appears after installation, check power, wiring, and LED indicators. Move the equipment outdoors for better GPS lock.
